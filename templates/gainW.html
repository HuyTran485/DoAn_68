Personalized Plans for Gaining or Losing Weight

Weight Gain Plan

Workouts:

  • Day 1: Bench press, Incline dumbbell press, Dumbbell fly, Tricep dips.
  • Day 2: Pull-ups, Barbell row, Lat pull-down, Bicep curls.
  • Day 3: Squats, Deadlift, Leg press, Walking lunges.
  • Day 4: Overhead press, Lateral raises, Front raises, Plank.

Nutrition:

  • Increase 500-1000 calories above maintenance level.
  • Protein: 20-30% (1.6-2g/kg body weight).
  • Carbohydrate: 50-60%.
  • Fat: 20-25%.
  • Sample Menu:
    • Breakfast: 2 eggs, 2 slices of whole-grain bread, peanut butter, 1 glass of whole milk.
    • Mid-morning: 1 banana, 1 protein bar.
    • Lunch: 150g chicken breast, 200g white rice, 100g green vegetables, 1 tablespoon olive oil.
    • Afternoon: 1 smoothie (avocado, banana, milk), 1 handful of almonds.
    • Dinner: 150g salmon, 200g sweet potato, mixed green salad.
    • Before bed: 1 glass of milk, 1 apple.
