Personalized Plans for Gaining or Losing Weight

Weight Loss Plan

Workouts:

  • Cardio: Running (30 minutes), Cycling (20 minutes), HIIT (20 seconds sprint + 40 seconds walk, repeat 10 times).
  • Strength Training:
    • Full Body: Squats, Push-ups, Dumbbell row.
    • Core and Legs: Plank, Side plank, Lunges.
    • Chest and Arms: Dumbbell press, Tricep dips, Bicep curls.

Nutrition:

  • Reduce 300-500 calories below maintenance level.
  • Protein: 25-30% (1.6-2g/kg body weight).
  • Carbohydrate: 40-50%.
  • Fat: 20-30%.
  • Sample Menu:
    • Breakfast: 2 boiled eggs, 1 slice of whole-grain bread, 1 cup of unsweetened green tea.
    • Mid-morning: 1 apple, 10 almonds.
    • Lunch: 100g chicken breast, 150g brown rice, 100g steamed green vegetables.
    • Afternoon: 1 cup of unsweetened yogurt, 1 strawberry.
    • Dinner: 150g white fish (steamed/grilled), mixed salad, 1 tablespoon olive oil.
    • Before bed: 1 glass of warm water.
